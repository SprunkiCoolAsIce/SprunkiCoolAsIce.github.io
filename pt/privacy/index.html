Política de Privacidade

Política de Privacidade da Sprunki Cool As Ice

Effective Date: December 13, 2024

Welcome to Sprunki Cool As Ice! This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website https://sprunkicoolasice.org.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: We collect your name, email, and payment information for order processing.
  • Non-Personal Information: We use web cookies to enhance your experience on our site.

Purpose of Data Collection

The personal data we collect is used solely for processing your orders and ensuring a smooth transaction experience.

Data Sharing

We respect your privacy and do not share your personal data with any third parties.

Children's Privacy

Our website does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and users will be notified through the website.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@sprunkicoolasice.org.

Thank you for visiting Sprunki Cool As Ice!
